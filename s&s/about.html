The KT 4 Legacy is part of Klay Thompson's signature line with Anta. It features a high-top design for added ankle support, a textured and patterned upper for durability, and shock-absorbing technology for responsive performance on the basketball court. The shoe is tailored for shooters with a smooth and stable fit.

Known for its vibrant and dynamic design, the Kyrie Shock Wave 5 is an Anta basketball shoe that emphasizes quick cuts and agility, much like Kyrie Irving's playstyle.
